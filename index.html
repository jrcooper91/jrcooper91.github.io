Current Projects

Jenn Cooper is a Madison & Lila Self Graduate Fellow & PhD Candidate in Physics at the University of Kansas. This is a snapshot of of her current involvement in a variety of projects and organizations.

Icon

Cosmic Environment & Galaxy Evolution

Life & death in the cosmos: how do dense environments such as galaxy clusters affect their ability to survive and create new stars?

Icon

Machine Learning in Astronomy

How can machine learning automate and improve our classification of the gas morphology of galaxies? Image classification is an efficient and accurate method.

2019-2020 APECS President

Jenn is currently the President of the Association of Polar Early Career Scientists, an organization that focuses on providing opportunities for early career polar scientists around the globe through capacity building, being on the cutting edge of information assessment, and education and outreach
